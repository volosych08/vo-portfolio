Why ICT

I became curious about technology when I was 13. My first experience was with Python, where I followed tutorials to build a simple calculator. Later, I studied C++ with a tutor and managed to create a small PC game — Snake. These small projects gave me the excitement of turning ideas into something real, and that feeling has stayed with me ever since

After returning to programming through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I realized that ICT is the perfect field for me. I enjoy solving problems, experimenting with new technologies, and learning independently from online resources. ICT allows me to combine creativity and logic while preparing me for a future where technology is essential in every part of life. This is why I chose the HBO-ICT program at HZ — it will help me turn my passion into professional skills.

Why HZ

Quality of education HZ is well known for applied sciences and ICT programs.
