FAQ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can print using the on-campus printers, typically located in the Mediatheek, Open Learning Centre (OLC), or library at both Middelburg and Vlissingen campuses. To pay for printing, you need funds on your HZ student card, which you can top up using a PinPoint machine located in the central hall near reception HZ University of Applied Sciences.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

HZ provides scanning facilities in the Open Learning Centre (OLC) on the ground floor of the Middelburg location HZ University of Applied Sciences.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

HZ offers a physical HZ Shop at both campus locations (F-corridor, Vlissingen; entrance, Middelburg) where you can buy stationery, HZ merchandise, and snacks.

How can you book a project space?

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

To park your car at HZ (Vlissingen only): You need your HZ student card to scan at the barrier (slagboom) to enter the campus
